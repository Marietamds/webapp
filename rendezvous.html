RENDEZ-VOUS

"Rendez-vous" is the name of a fictional restaurant located in the center of Paris, which takes inspiration from what nature offers each season, exploring French haute cuisine and enhancing the product by blending tradition with innovation.

This project was created to apply everything learned about the fundamentals of UX/UI design. It involves designing and prototyping a responsive website adapted for mobile, tablet, and desktop formats, based on User-Centered Design (UCD) and following the Mobile First design model.

To do this, I decided to create the visual identity of the restaurant, and based on research into the industry and its competitors, I could properly define its logo, color palette, typography, and photography direction.

Brand identity, design system & UX/UI.

Made in 2024.
