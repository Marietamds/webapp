COVER OF THE COVER

Music is a great source of inspiration in my life and in my work as a designer. Every time I listen to a new album, I think about how the cover could have been designed differently. Throughout this page, I will showcase my redesign of the aesthetic concept of different albums and musical stages of the artist Post Malone, including vinyl packaging and posters.

This first album is about AUSTIN, the penultimate one in which he experiments with pop music. I wanted to respect the use of the original typography and keep two of the most characteristic colors from the cover.

Illustration, graphic design,
visual identity & photograph edition.

Made in 2024.
