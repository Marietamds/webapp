O'LIVE

O'LIVE is a university branding project that was born from the need to create an organic cosmetics brand taking advantage of our country's abundant resources that may initially seem disposable.

I did a research about new possible ways of taking part in helping the environment and people at the same time. Since I found out that Spain is one of the biggest producers of olives, that have many benefits that we aren't taking advantage of, I decided to create biodegradable products with natural ingredients made from olive stone biomaterial.

In this way, we not only collaborate to build a more sustainable future but we also support an activity as typical, artisanal and cultural in our country as agriculture and olive harvesting.

Strategy, tone of voice, brand identity, design system & illustration.

Made in 2024.
