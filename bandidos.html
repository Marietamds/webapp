BANDIDOS DEL AMOR

"Bandidos del amor" is a personal illustration project in which I get inspiration from different songs by the band Peach Tree Rascals to create posters reflecting what these songs convey to me.

In this series, I try to experiment by mixing analog drawing with the possibilities offered by the digital medium. I aim to express as much as possible through the color palette, typography, and textures.
